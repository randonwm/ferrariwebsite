Ferrari F2001

Engineered for dominance, driven to perfection.

Car Overview

The Ferrari F2001 was a masterpiece of speed and efficiency. Driven by Michael Schumacher during the 2001 Formula One season, it led Ferrari to yet another championship with 9 wins and unmatched reliability.

It featured a powerful V10 engine and pioneering aerodynamics, especially the high-nose chassis that later influenced the grid.

Key Specs

  • Engine: 3.0 L V10
  • Power: 825 hp @ 17,300 rpm
  • Transmission: 7-speed semi-automatic
  • Top Speed: 370 km/h

Technical Highlights

Chassis

Carbon fibre and honeycomb composite structure

Suspension

Independent push-rod, torsion spring suspension

Brakes

Carbon-fibre discs with Brembo calipers

Fuel

Shell V-Power Racing Fuel
