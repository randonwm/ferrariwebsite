Ferrari SF-25

Ferrari’s 2025 challenger, designed for innovation, precision, and power — led by the iconic red team’s mission to reclaim F1 dominance.

Technical Specifications

  • Engine: 1.6 L Turbocharged V6 Hybrid (Ferrari 066/10)
  • Total Power: ~1000 hp (ICE + ERS)
  • Transmission: 8-speed semi-automatic
  • Top Speed: 350+ km/h
  • ERS: MGU-K + MGU-H (energy recovery systems)
  • Weight: 798 kg (with driver & fluids)

The SF-25 combines cutting-edge hybrid technology and refined aerodynamics to meet the 2025 FIA regulations. Driven by Charles Leclerc and his teammate, this machine is Ferrari's bid to return to world championship glory with improved energy efficiency, handling, and raw speed.
