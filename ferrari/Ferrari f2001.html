Ferrari F2001

One of Ferrari’s most dominant Formula 1 cars, the F2001 powered Michael Schumacher to a Drivers’ Championship and helped secure the Constructors’ title during the 2001 season.

Technical Specifications

  • Engine: 3.0 L V10 (Ferrari Tipo 050)
  • Power: 825 hp @ 17,300 rpm
  • Transmission: 7-speed semi-automatic
  • Top Speed: 370 km/h
  • Weight: 600 kg (with driver and fluids)

The Ferrari F2001 was designed by Rory Byrne and featured improved aerodynamics, a lower center of gravity, and one of the most powerful engines of its time. Michael Schumacher and Rubens Barrichello drove it to 9 wins and a dominant season.
