Scuderia Ferrari

Passion. Heritage. Excellence.

Team Principal: Fred Vasseur

Fred Vasseur joined Ferrari in 2023 after a successful career in various roles within motorsport. He previously worked as the Team Principal for Alfa Romeo Racing, and his expertise includes managing both teams and drivers in F1. Vasseur is known for his strategic mind, leadership skills, and hands-on approach, making him a respected figure in motorsport. His leadership at Ferrari is seen as a crucial element in rebuilding the team and aiming for more success in Formula 1's competitive environment.

The Team

From engineers to drivers, every member of Scuderia Ferrari embodies dedication and passion. Together, they strive to uphold the team's illustrious history and push the boundaries of racing excellence.

Our Fans: The Tifosi

The Tifosi are the heartbeat of Ferrari. Their unwavering support and fervent passion fuel the team's drive, making every race a testament to their loyalty.

Legacy of Champions

With 16 Constructors' Championships and 15 Drivers' Championships, Ferrari's legacy is etched in the annals of Formula 1 history. The team's commitment to excellence continues to inspire generations.
