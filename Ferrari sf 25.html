Ferrari SF-25

Designed for precision. Built for championship glory.

Car Overview

The SF-25 is Scuderia Ferrari’s flagship machine for the 2025 Formula 1 season, showcasing advanced hybrid power, sleek aerodynamics, and a design refined in Maranello's wind tunnel. With a mission to reclaim dominance, the SF-25 delivers balance, speed, and control in every corner.

Engineered with cutting-edge materials and insights from years of racing innovation, this car represents the future of Formula 1 at Ferrari.

Key Specs

  • Engine: 1.6 L Turbocharged V6 Hybrid
  • Total Power: ~1000 hp (with ERS)
  • Transmission: 8-speed semi-automatic
  • Top Speed: 350+ km/h

Technical Highlights

Chassis

Carbon-fibre composite with monocoque structure

Suspension

Push-rod front / pull-rod rear geometry

Power Unit

Ferrari 066/10 Hybrid Turbo V6

Braking

Carbon discs with regenerative braking integration
